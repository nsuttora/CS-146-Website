About Us

Many incoming freshman feel overwhelmed when first entering college due to large class loads, difficult course material, lack of time, and an overall different and daunting experience. Due to these problems, many freshman struggle in their course work, receive poor grades, and foster a negative mental health. Ultimately, many freshman need to seek help and advice. We are here to solve this problem! We are a small group of college students who decided to create a website that accurately helps other college students succeed in college. Due to our varying questions in order to gauge student strengths and weaknessess, students can receive personalized tips that will surely help them succeed.
