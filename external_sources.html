External Sources


Having Trouble Academically?

  • Khan Academy

    Khan Academy is a great resource for practice problems and academic videos that cover wide range of subjects. After a free sign-up, you will have immediate access to help.

  • Stevens Undergraduate Academic Support Center

    The Academic Support Center provides a wide range of academic services to undergraduate students. Services such as quiz & exam reviews and workshops on study skills and time management strategies are offered.

    Location: Office of Undergraduate Academics on the 9th floor of Howe Center.

  • Requesting a Tutor

    Through myStevens intranet, students can complete and submit a "Tutor Request" form to request a tutor for free. Tutoring is only offered to undergraduate students. Tutoring is not provided for Humanities courses.

    All tutors are randomly assigned.


Having Trouble Emotionally?

  • Counseling & Psychological Services

    Stevens Counseling and Psychological Services provides free and confidential personalized counseling to students enrolled in Stevens.

    Location: Howe Center, 7th Floor Hours: Monday-Friday, 9am - 5pm Tuesdays from 5pm - 7pm (by appointment only) Phone: 201.216.5177

  • Online Mental Health Sreening

    The Online Mental Health Screening provided by ULifeline is a free and confidential online self evaluator. However, the Self Evaluator, while evidence-based, is for informational and educational purposes only and is not intended to

    be a substitute for professional diagnosis and treatment.
